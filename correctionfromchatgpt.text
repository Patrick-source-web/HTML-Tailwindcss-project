What changed & why:
grid-cols-[1fr_1fr] → two equal flexible columns (so you don’t have to guess exact widths).

max-w-6xl mx-auto → caps overall width and centers the grid container.

gap-x-12 → space between columns.

py-16 → vertical padding top & bottom so image/text aren’t flush.

On the text side:

flex flex-col justify-center → vertically center the text content in its cell.

px-8 → padding inside so text doesn’t touch edges.

On the image side:

flex justify-center items-start → start (top) alignment inside cell, and ability to add bottom padding.

pb-6 on image → bottom space is small but present.

max-w-full object-contain → prevents overflow and preserves aspect ratio.
